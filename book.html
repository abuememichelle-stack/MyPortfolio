Booking FAQs

Q How long is the consultation?
The initial consultation is 30–45 minutes. This gives us enough time to discuss your needs and identify the best path forward without overwhelming you with information overload.

It helps to have a rough idea of your goals, the current tools or systems you're using, and any specific challenges you want to address. Nothing formal required — just come as you are.
